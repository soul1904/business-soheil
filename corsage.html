Project Description

This application is a preferred destination for users who are looking to locate the best option regarding a tattoo shop, related to their prefered style, according to their location. Using our suggested links the users can browse for the most suitable tattoo shop that specializes in their preferred tattoo style, while the website uses a geolocator to pinpoint the top rated shops in the usersâ€™ location. The users can also look for a few other popular styles and be able to compare them before committing to a life-long decision. In conclusion, we want to make the whole process of searching for tattoo styles and finding a proper shop nearby more streamlined.
